What is Bornahead

Bornahead is a world that is ahead of ours — more advanced in technology, in systems, in power. A civilization that has evolved far beyond what we know, with its own history, politics, conflicts, and stories. A universe that is, in every sense, born ahead of our own.

The Bornahead Cinematic Universe (BCU) is the connected story world that every Waarko Studio game exists within. No single main character. No single villain. A living world where multiple stories unfold across multiple games — all connected, all part of something larger than any one title.
